So... What's Climate Change anyway?

Climate Change refers to long term shifts in weather patterns and temperatures.

Although these shifts can happen naturally, such as during solar events, they have mostly been caused by Human Activities.

In fact, greenhouse gas concentrations are at its highest levels in over 2 million years!
