small solutions to solve
our biggest problem

scenario 1:

AT HOME

1

Green Energy!

Switch to more energy efficient appliances and contact your utility provider about green power schemes

2

Green Thumbs!

Grow your own vegetables and produce at home! Not only is it fun to do gardening as a hobby, it reduces your carbon footprint as well!

3

Green Diet?

Did you know that the carbon footprint of eating a cheeseburger is equal to 15 salads? Consuming less red meat and dairy is not only good for the environment, but your health too!

4

Bicycle by Chungha

By Cycling, not only do you get more exercise, you can also reduce the amount of carbon dioxide emitted from the burning of fossil fuels.

5

Donate to Charity!

If you have the funds for it, why not donate to charity? There are a few reputable charities, such as WWF or CRF.

6

Talk & Talk

Talk about climate change more with people! By engaging in conversations, we can spread awareness and get more attention on this issue.
